About Us

A non-governmental organization for welfare in the education field. Plays an important role in supporting the most vulnerable children to access their right to education through innovative ideas such as forming student groups and child-tracking systems, holding awareness workshops with parents to ensure that they send children to school. Also provides placement & apprenticeship opportunities to ITI, BTech, and diploma students for their bright future.
